About Project

This static website is designed to cater the digital marketing needs of consumers across the world. Below are the components that played the important part in bringing together this website.

  • - Responsiveness: This website is responsive to screens of any desktop, mobile and tablet. for this we used media queries in css and meta viewport tag

  • - Usability: We created tabs with easy to understand tab headings to navigate across pages and website. The page content is logically separated by tabs.

  • - Content: The theme of our website is Digital Marketing. It will serve as a platform for customer's bright idea for their business growth and marketing.

    • Rohit Kumar

  • - Design:
    1) Simplicity and Visual Hierarchy: The website is designed in a manner so that the visitors will naturally gravitates towards the most important element first.

    2) Consistency: The website is designed in a manner so that all of the web pages have similar consistency in terms of font, background, colors, content and responsiveness.

    3) Conventionality and User Centricity: The website has been designed keeping in mind the conventionality of website development. Also, it is user centric to provide best experience for visitors.
